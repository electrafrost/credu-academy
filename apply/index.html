Apply to CREDU Academy

Cohorts are limited to ten to preserve build velocity and mentoring depth.

Before applying, consider

  • You will remain professionally active during the residency
  • You are prepared to build and execute on something that matters to you
  • You can commit consistent weekly time (minimum 10 hours) for three months
  • You are comfortable working alongside highly autonomous peers

How it works

All assessment is self-assessment. We're not gatekeeping your competence — we're looking for people who are honest about where they are and serious about where they're going.

  1. Submit the application form below
  2. Shortlisted applicants are invited to a conversation
  3. Offers are issued on a rolling basis

If accepted, you'll receive onboarding details, an invoice requiring payment to reserve your place, visa guidance and preparatory reading.
