The programme

Your programme, your path

This isn't a fixed curriculum. It's a choose-your-own-adventure programme built around your expertise, what you want to build, and where you want to take your practice - with structured mentoring and a cohort of no more than 10.

Network School runs a rich programme of talks, workshops and events covering AI, decentralisation technologies, the digital economy and healthy living. You participate in all of that. Around it, we tailor the CREDU Academy experience for each cohort - presenting ideas, building projects, and working toward outcomes to improve our professional futures, together.

Applications are selective. We want high-agency, curious people ready to co-create the future of the profession. This isn't a passive course - it's a working residency.

Month 1

Orient, Foundation & Discovery

  • Settle into Forest City life, join community programming, set up remote work routines
  • Define your domain expertise, where it intersects with emerging tech, and set goals with your cohort and mentor
  • Define your project - whether that's an AI use case, tech solution, or professional practice business model
  • Build your first working application with AI-assisted coding - or get technical feedback and advance an existing project
  • Foundations in blockchain, crypto accounting, decentralised operations, and foreign tax concepts
Month 2

Deepen, Build & Challenge

  • Further research and application of what was of most interest in Month 1
  • AI safety, governance and strategy for your profession and client industries
  • Critical assessment of current tools and vendor software, explore emerging tools
  • International tax practice, cross-border structures and evaluating AI-driven systems
  • Digital assets deep dive - use cases, classification, security, and reporting frameworks
  • Design a DAO - using blockchain technology to enable decentralised governance and decision-making
  • Network state concepts examined through a regulatory and tax lens
Month 3

Integrate, Ship & Contribute

This month will be shaped around the specific interests of the cohort. May include:

  • Further developing and applying what we learned in Month 2
  • Build your professional practice action plan, based on assumptions about what's coming next - what you'll be working on in 1, 3 and 5 years, and how you're getting there
  • Finalise and present projects with a roadmap to take them further
  • Establish cross-border collaboration networks and a verified professional profile that markets your specialisation
  • Transnational tax coordination as a cohort policy project for network states
  • Contribute to development of a decentralised credentialing and peer-to-peer CPD model

Throughout all 3 months

  • Participate in Network School programming - talks, workshops and community events
  • Maintain remote work or professional study commitments
  • Weekly cohort sessions and collaborative working in the faculty co-working space
  • Ongoing mentoring and one-to-one guidance from Electra Frost and other industry leaders
  • All CPD hours tracked and logged from day one
What you leave with

Programme outcomes

  • A completed project - tool, framework or validated business model
  • Practical ability to build software using AI-assisted and agentic coding tools
  • Digital assets fluency - use cases, classification, security and reporting
  • AI safety, governance and strategy literacy for the profession
